01 · Introduction

What Is Wish Coin?

Wish Coin ($WISH) is a community-driven digital currency designed to democratize access to cryptocurrency through mobile mining. Built on the belief that financial tools should be accessible to everyone — not just those with expensive hardware — Wish Coin allows everyday users to accumulate a meaningful digital asset simply by using the Wish mining application.

The core philosophy behind Wish Coin is simplicity, fairness, and long-term value creation. No complex rigs. No massive upfront investment. Just your phone, your community, and your belief in the future of decentralized finance.

With a fixed total supply of 950,000,000 $WISH, the tokenomics are designed to balance accessibility during the mining phase with scarcity as the ecosystem matures — positioning early miners as the backbone of the Wish economy.

04 · Mining Mechanism

How Mobile Mining Works

The Wish mining app uses a lightweight, phone-friendly model that rewards active participation over computational power. Unlike proof-of-work systems requiring energy-intensive hardware, $WISH mining is based on activity-based validation — your daily engagement with the app is proof of your contribution to the network.

Once points are converted to $WISH tokens, users can send and receive them to anyone in the world through the fully functional Wish Wallet — internal wallet-to-wallet transfers, entirely within the Wish ecosystem, with no third-party app required.

Mining sessions are time-limited (4-hour sessions at up to 2,000 pts/hr) and must be manually initiated, ensuring that coin distribution is tied to genuine human participation. The mining rate adjusts over time as total supply approaches its cap, creating natural scarcity heading into the Q4 2027 deadline.

Upon deployment, $WISH will be issued as a standard blockchain token, enabling full interoperability with the broader DeFi ecosystem, wallets, and exchanges worldwide. The smart contract address will be announced officially — always verify through our official app and channels.
